Santorini, Greece


It's a beautiful island known for its stunning views of the Aegean Sea, white-washed buildings with blue domes, and breathtaking sunsets. It's a popular destination for couples and honeymooners, but it also offers a variety of activities for families and solo travelers, such as exploring the ancient ruins of Akrotiri, hiking along the caldera, and enjoying the local cuisine and wine.
